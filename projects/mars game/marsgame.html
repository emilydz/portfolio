Mission to Mars

Game Design / Graphic Design

What will it take to get to Mars? How will we get there? What challenges will we face along the way?

Mission to Mars is a board game that explores the perils of deep space travel. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration in trying to solve some of today's most difficult questions.
In this cooperative game, you are crew members on a mission to Mars. Sending astronauts to Mars and returning them safely to Earth is a challenging and dangerous mission, fraught with hazards.
